Transforming Potential into Independence

Ashabhavan established in the year 1993 and it stands to empower the marginalized and differently abled girls and bring them in to the mainstream of the society. Today Ashabhavan has become a shelter and power to the weak and downtrodden all around. It is our ambition to help them themselves, guide them to lead a disciplined, instruct them and set goals to enable them to secure an independent and happy existence. Subsequently we hope to mould their personalities into responsible citizens, proud of themselves, self-confident powerful individuals. Ashabhavan girls are talented in various ways and do display them in numerous fields they are engaged. They are acquiring training in the Fashion designing and Garment technology for a govt. approved certificate course. Under the Indian Computer Education Society MS OFFICE, DTP and PSC recognized CBC (Computer Basic Course) courses are also furnished. For those who cannot cope with the above mentioned courses. Courses like tailoring and embroidery are offered.
