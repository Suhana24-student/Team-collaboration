Courses We Offer

Asha Bhavan is conducting various courses for the disabled girls to achieve the goal of self-sufficiency in life. It will help to get job in Govt. or private sector those who completed the courses. Most of our students are deaf and dumb, mild mentally retarded and orthopedically handicapped. We are giving them training in Computer, Tailoring and Embroidery, Fashion Designing & Garment technology in short FDGT.
