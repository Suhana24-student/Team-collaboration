Our Courses

What We Offer

.

Computer Courses

We place strong emphasis on computer education as a key component of skill development courses such as MS Office, Desktop Publishing (DTP), and English and Malayalam typing. we offer courses are designed to equip them with essential digital literacy skills relevant to today’s professional environment.We also provide structured exam preparation to ensure our students are well-prepared for certification. We are proud to note that our computer training program is recognized by the Indian Computer Academy, further enhancing the credibility and value of the course.

Tailoring Course

Hand on skills training includes master in garment construction, alteration and repair techniques. Precision and attention to detail and develop fine motor skills. The main career paths we intended to our trainees to be a tailor and alteration specialist. We firstly assessed the current level of the students and set a plan and started training. Termly and timely evaluations and monitoring made the training programes more effective and fruitful.

Embroidery

Embroidery has its traditional importance. It can be calming and meditative, promoting relaxation and focus. Add value to garments and made attractive stitching patterns.These courses will help these students to achieve self-sufficiency in life and secure job in private and public sector. And it will help reach of the mainstream of the society.

Fashion Designing & Garment Technology Course

Our Fashion designing course is shortly known as FDGT. Develop skills in garment construction, textile science and fashion illustration.learn about current trends, patterns and designs in the fashion industry.Gets to pursue careers in fashion designing and styling. In public sector schools there are posted as craft teacher. The course affiliation is from technical education for fashion designing and garment technology (KGTE -FDGT).
